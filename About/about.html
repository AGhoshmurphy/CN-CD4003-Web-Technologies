ABOUT US

Hi Folks,

Welcome to our online repair shop, where we specialize in restoring high-quality vintage items from the 60’s, 70’s, 80’s and 90’s. We offer a wide range of services to help you extend the life of your favorite vintage treasures by repairing them instead of replacing them.

Our team of expert technicians has years of experience in restoring a wide variety of vintage goods including watches, clothes, white goods, vintage cars, bicycles, bags, silverware, jewellery, paintings, LPs, VCRs, classic board games, video games, classic toys, and much more. At our online repair shop, we understand the importance of preserving our planet and its resources. That's why we encourage our customers to choose repairing over replacing their vintage items to reduce waste and promote sustainability. We provide a cost-effective solution that is better for both the environment and your wallet. We strive to provide excellent customer service and quality repairs that last. Our efficient process ensures that your item is repaired and returned to you as quickly as possible. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail and our commitment to exceeding our customers' expectations.

While you're here, be sure to check out our eBay outlet, where we offer a selection of refurbished vintage items for sale. We hope you enjoy shopping at our online store and look forward to helping you restore your favorite vintage items.
